Upcoming Swimming Competition

We are excited to announce that a swimming competition will be held at our sport facilities on 15/11/2024. The event will feature a variety of races, including freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and relay events for all skill levels. Participants are required to arrive at least 30 minutes before their scheduled race time for warm-up and registration. Spectators are welcome to cheer on the athletes, with seating available around the pool. Please ensure that all participants bring appropriate swim gear and arrive ready to compete!

New Waterpolo Program for Young Children

We are excited to announce the launch of a new waterpolo program for young children starting at 6. This introductory line will focus on basic skills, teamwork, and water safety in a fun and supportive environment. Practices will be held at our sport facilities every Monday and Wednesday from 5pm. Registration is now open, and spaces are limited. Join us and help your child discover the joy of waterpolo!

Granada Waterpolo Women's Team Wins National Championship!

We are thrilled to announce that the Granada Waterpolo Women's Team has won the National Championship! After an exciting and hard-fought season , our team triumphed in the final match, showcasing exceptional skill and teamwork. Congratulations to all the players, coaches, and supporters who made this victory possible. We are incredibly proud of their achievement and look forward to celebrating this remarkable accomplishment. Thank you to everyone for your continued support!
